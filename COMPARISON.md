## Unique "cvr.name.coder" Advantages

1.  **The Dreamer Engine**: A background process that "sleeps" over your conversation history to distill it into long-term facts, preventing context window bloat while keeping critical decisions alive forever.
2.  **Predictive Step Execution**: Instead of just doing what it's told, cvr.name.coder analyzes the project structure to predict the next 3 steps needed.
3.  **Visual Semantic Heatmap**: An interactive dashboard showing which parts of your project the agent is currently "thinking" about.
